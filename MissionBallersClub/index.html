About

We are a dedicated organization seeking to educate elementary and high school kids about the fundamentals of basketball. Our goal is to improve each and every one of our students' physical fitness and basketball ability. We hope that with our training, students will enjoy the sport of basketball as much as we do and achieve their goals, whether that be to improve their well-being or be on the school basketball team. Above all, we strive to create a comfortable environment and have fun!


Meet the Team

Founder and Lead Instructor - Lakshay Soin

Lakshay Soin is a 10th grader at Mission San Jose High School who is passionate about basketball and computer science. He has been playing competitive basketball since 1st grade and is currently on his school’s junior varsity basketball team. In his free time, Lakshay enjoys coding and playing basketball with his friends.
